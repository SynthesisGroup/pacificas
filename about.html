About Us

We believe in crafting pieces where sustainability and style go hand in hand.

Pacificas Wood Furniture is a furniture manufacturing company located in the heart of Clark, Pampanga.

Owned by a couple, Robert and Regina Annecston, it was named after Pacifica, California where the owners were originally from.

The journey of Pacificas started when the couple met with a master craftsman, Kaye. It came from conversations with Kaye who had many years of experience in woodworking and whose love for woodwork never faltered over the years, it blossomed into an idea to start a company fueled by passion and driven by their love to give back to the people.

The heart and soul of Pacificas Wood Furniture are all about creating quality masterpieces and their unflickering hope to help others achieve a good life and better opportunities.

Their love of quality shows in the furniture pieces that they masterfully create and it transcends to the quality of how they treat their people. The core of the business is to help people and give back to society by providing opportunities and jobs for Filipinos. It is their mantra to continuously grow the business so they can create more jobs for the underprivileged who were severely affected by the pandemic.

About the Owners

The power couple, Robert and Regina Annecston are no newbies when it comes to doing business.

They have been in the industry for quite some time now, and their dynamics as couple and business partners are what made their ventures a success. They are no two peas in a pod. In fact, they are quite opposite from one another which makes them more effective when it comes to managing and handling their business affairs. They play on each other's strengths and support one another to create a highly-efficient partnership fueled by their love for woodwork and their heart to help create more opportunities for the Filipinos.

Robert Annecston has more than three decades of experience in handling the technical side of the business. He is an expert when it comes to technical matters - from tools to styles and design. With years under his belt as a stagehand, you can say that he knows the ins and outs when it comes to tools. He knows what goes on behind the scene and he has the technical knowledge in the creation of the types of furniture that they make. Robert makes sure that each and every piece of furniture that they will be releasing is with his seal of quality.

Regina Annecston is a people person. and comes from a family of well-known artists and furniture makers.

She has an eye for detail and design which makes her a towering figure in the furniture industry. Aside from being born with a natural eye for arts, she also has more than 25 years of experience in scouting for talents and hiring the best person for a job, she is an expert in reading people that works for her. This comes in very handy as it was Regina who discovered Kaye. She noticed Kaye's talent in woodwork and knew that she has come across a craftsman who is an expert in his field and who has a talent for creating beautiful masterpieces. She is very hands-on when it comes to their business, she makes sure that their business runs smoothly and seamlessly. Each and every process of their business flow is accounted for.

Mission

Pacificas Wood Furniture aims to create quality masterpieces that are proudly Filipino-made. We are driven by our mission to become a one-stop shop for furniture solutions. And, we are fueled by our passion to empower the Filipinos families by growing the business to create more jobs and opportunities for our workers and their families.

Vision

To be a successful and growing furniture brand locally made in the Philippines. In the process of growing our business, we envision Pacificas Wood Furniture to provide jobs for the Filipinos while creative quality furniture pieces.
